About

RiskHaz is an online calculator that takes data published by the EPA and calculates human health risk and hazard values for concentrations of environmental compounds in soil. Users select the regulator, receptor, and pathways they are interested in, enter their analytes and concentrations, and the site returns the risk and hazard values associated with the selected analytes. Environmental consultants and regulators may find this site useful. Toxicity values and equations were obtained from the EPA and California Department of Toxic Substances Control.
